Payment Policy

  1. Delivery charges vary per product. Most products offer free delivery in India.
  2. Delivery time: 7 working days for domestic, 15–20 for international. Orders ship in 4–5 days.
  3. We use top courier services: BlueDart, Aramex, DTDC, DHL, FedEx. If not serviceable, India Post is used.
  4. All items are securely packed to avoid damage during transit.
  5. Multiple payment options: Internet Banking, Debit/Credit Cards, and Cash on Delivery available.
  6. We accept VISA, MasterCard, American Express & Club Cards. All data encrypted via secure gateways.
  7. Basket never stores card details. We use 256-bit SSL encryption through trusted partners like EBS/CCAvenue.

Return Policy

  1. Returns accepted within 7 days of delivery if the product is damaged or incorrect.
  2. To request a return, contact customer support with order ID and images of the issue.
  3. Products must be unused, with original packaging and invoice to be eligible for return.
  4. Refunds are processed within 7–10 working days after inspection.
  5. For COD orders, bank details must be shared for refund processing.

Privacy Policy

  1. Your personal information is secure and never shared without your consent.
  2. We collect data such as name, email, address for order processing and communication.
  3. All transactions are encrypted and protected via SSL for your safety.
  4. You can access or delete your data anytime by contacting our support.
  5. Our website may use cookies to improve user experience and personalize content.
